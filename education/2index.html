NEW EDUCATION CURRICULUM

Initially before the implementation of the new curriculum,there were other three education systems that existed before. namely;

  1. East African Community(7-4-2-3)system which involved the three East African countries.
  2. kenya 7-4-2-3 sytem which was continued by Kenya after the collapse of E.A Community
  3. 8-4-4 curriculum which was introduced by President Daniel Torotich Arap Moi

2-6-3-3-3 syastem of Education.

The new curriculumhas been toutedas the ultimate remedy to limitations identified in the 8-4-4 system because it is entirely skills based.

Itwas piloted last year between may and September across 470 schools.

The 2-6-3-3-3 model places emphasis on formative years of learning where learners will spend total of eight years-2 in pre-primary and six in primary

Subjects to be taught in lower primary are:

  1. Kiswahili
  2. English
  3. Literacy
  4. mother tongue
  5. science
  6. social studies
  7. Agricultural activities

Upper primary subjects are:

  1. Kiswahili
  2. English
  3. Mathematics
  4. Home science
  5. Agriculture
  6. science and Technology
  7. Moral and life skills
  8. Physical and Health
